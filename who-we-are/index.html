Our Mission

To Create a Healthy & Happy World.

Everest came into existence in 2001 & got formally recognized and registered on January 1, 2014 with the Government of India, with our head office in Ludhiana, Punjab (India).

At Everest Yoga & Pilates Institute, we teach yoga as a combination of "TRADITIONAL YOGA & PHILOSOPHY" and "MODERN YOGIC SCIENCE".

We coach Pilates using a variety of Equipments and props -Mat, Reformer, Wunda Chair, Caformer, Spine Corrector, Ladder Barrel, Motor Pilates.

Our yoga & pilates training works on all layers of an individual- PHYSICAL HEALTH, EMOTIONAL STABILITY, MENTAL BALANCE, SOCIAL GROWTH & SPIRITUAL ELEVATION.

Our Contribution

Mission Vijay Corona & Ghar Ghar Yoga

We effectively launched programs - MISSION VIJAY CORONA & GHAR GHAR YOGA during pandemic COVID 19 in March 2020. This was an online program where we connected yoga leaders, yoga teachers, health care workers & healers from all over the world and everyone rendered their services on our online platform. The intention behind the program was to unconditionally serve humanity to stay balanced, while the entire human race was restricted within the four walls.

CONDUCTING INTERNATIONAL YOGA FESTIVAL since 2021

Since 2021, we have been hosting an Online Yoga Festival bringing holistic wisdom to a global audience. Experts in Yoga, Ayurveda, and Naturopathy lead transformative online sessions. We unite spiritual teachers, kirtankaars, singers, healers, and dieticians on one platform. Our mission is to share diverse knowledge for complete well-being. Supporting mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual health remains at our core.
