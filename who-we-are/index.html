Our Philosophy

“योगः कर्मसु कौशलम्”

Bhagavad Gita 2.50 — Yoga is skill in action

Institute is a firm follower of Karma Yoga. We believe in unselfish action, performed with mindfulness and awareness. For us, Yoga is not merely physical exercise, but a scientific approach to harmonize the physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual dimensions of life.

Our philosophy is rooted in the ancient wisdom of India and supported by modern yogic science. We embrace the principle that yoga is for everyone — regardless of age, background, or ability — and we adapt practices to individual needs through thoughtful modifications and variations.

At Institute, Yoga is both a discipline and a lifestyle, cultivating balance, inner strength, and resilience. Guided by our shlokas and values, we strive to carry forward the timeless tradition of yoga as a tool for self-transformation and global harmony.

Our History

Institute came into existence in 2014 and was formally recognized in 2016 under the Indian Societies Act 1860. Since then, it has grown into a global hub of yogic training and research, spreading authentic practices worldwide.
