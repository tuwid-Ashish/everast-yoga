Program Overview

The 500-hour certification integrates the complete 200H foundation with the advanced 300H sequence. Choose residential or non-residential format; both deliver the same rigorous syllabus and assessment standards over an intensive 25-day cohort.

  • Full classical mat & equipment repertoire
  • Applied anatomy & biomechanics for movement
  • Professional cueing, class design & safety
  • Practice teaching, observation & feedback
