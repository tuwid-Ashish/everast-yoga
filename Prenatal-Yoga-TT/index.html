Prenatal Yoga Teacher Training

Supporting the sacred journey of motherhood through the wisdom of yoga. Our comprehensive program prepares you to guide expecting mothers with safety, compassion, and expertise through every stage of pregnancy and beyond.

Program Objectives

To equip participants with safe prenatal yoga techniques, deepen awareness of pregnancy and postpartum needs, and prepare couples and communities to create a nurturing environment for both mother and child. This program blends yoga, meditation, breathwork, and lifestyle practices to support mothers-to-be and their families.

Comprehensive Training Modules

15 specialized modules covering every aspect of prenatal yoga and care

Benefits of Yogic Techniques During Pregnancy

Explore how yoga supports physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being throughout pregnancy, enhancing maternal health and fetal development.

Anatomical & Physiological Changes

Understanding the remarkable transformations happening in a woman's body during pregnancy and how to adapt yoga practice accordingly.

Conditions, Complications & Cautions

Learn to identify pregnancy conditions, understand complications, and master essential contraindications for safe practice.

Major Muscles in Pregnancy

Focus on key muscle groups that require attention during pregnancy, including pelvic floor, core, and postural muscles.

Trimester-Wise Asana Instructions

Comprehensive guide to safe and effective yoga postures for each trimester, with modifications and progressions.

Breech Baby

Specialized techniques and positions to help encourage optimal fetal positioning and address breech presentation.

Labor & Birth Process

Understanding the physiology and stages of labor and birth, with yoga techniques to support natural delivery.

Yogic Philosophy for Pregnancy

Ancient wisdom and modern understanding of how yogic philosophy supports the journey of pregnancy and motherhood.

Garbh Sanskar

Traditional practices for prenatal education and spiritual connection with the unborn child, fostering optimal development.

Psychological Considerations

Supporting mental and emotional health during pregnancy, addressing anxiety, mood changes, and psychological well-being.

Nutrition in Pregnancy

Essential nutritional guidance for expecting mothers, including dietary recommendations and supplements for optimal health.

Asanas in Pregnancy

Safe and effective yoga postures specifically adapted for pregnant women, with detailed instructions and modifications.

Pranayama in Pregnancy

Breathing techniques for pregnancy, labor preparation, stress relief, and connecting with the growing baby.

Mudras in Pregnancy

Sacred hand gestures and their healing applications during pregnancy for energy balancing and spiritual connection.

Yoga Nidra in Pregnancy

Deep relaxation techniques for stress relief, better sleep, and creating positive connections with the unborn child.

The Fourth Trimester

Postpartum care and recovery, supporting new mothers through the crucial period after birth with gentle yoga, healing practices, and emotional support. Understanding the physical and psychological changes in the weeks following delivery and how yoga can aid in recovery, bonding, and adaptation to motherhood.
