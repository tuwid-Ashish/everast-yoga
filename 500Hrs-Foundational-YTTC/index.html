Advanced Program Objectives

The objectives of the 500 Hours Professional Yoga Teacher Training Course are to provide participants with advanced knowledge and expertise in yoga practice, philosophy, anatomy, and teaching methodology. The course is designed to deepen personal practice, enhance teaching skills, and equip trainees to conduct multi-level, specialized, and therapy-oriented yoga classes safely and effectively. It also emphasizes professional ethics, leadership, and personal transformation, preparing graduates to contribute meaningfully to the yoga community and uphold the standards of a professional yoga teacher.

Training Modules

500 Hour Detailed Syllabus (Yoga Alliance aligned)

1

YOGA MODULE 1: ASANAS

Core Practice

We teach traditional yogasanas (Raj Yoga, Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga) as well as modern forms of yoga which are taught at level 1, 2, 3, 4 & 5. These levels are based on beginner, intermediate & advanced level of practice.

Everest’s self-created series of asanas based on seven spinal movements are absolutely noteworthy. Hot Yoga, Yin Yoga, Restorative Yoga, Athletic Yoga, Rhythmic Yoga, Artistic Yoga and many more forms are taught. Yoga with a variety of props makes our training courses unique.

2

YOGA MODULE 2: PRANAYAMA

Breathwork

Comprehensive training in the science and practice of pranayama (breath control). Students learn various traditional breathing techniques that form the foundation of advanced yogic practices, with emphasis on breath awareness and control, integration with asana practice, and therapeutic applications.

3

YOGA MODULE 3: MUDRA, BANDHA, DRISHTI

Advanced Techniques

Understanding and implementation of mudras, bandhas & drishtis during the training takes the student forward through the yogic practices. The module covers hand mudras and their significance, energy locks (bandhas), focused gaze techniques (drishti), and how to integrate these within personal and teaching practice.

4

YOGA MODULE 4: SHATKARMAS

Cleansing Practices

Cleansing of the physical body is primary in yogic practice. We teach objectives, methods & precautions of detoxification practices, including Jal Neti and Rubber Neti, Shankprakshalan, Kunjal and Kapal Bhati, Tratak and other traditional cleansing techniques.

5

YOGA MODULE 5: YOGIC HUMANITIES

Philosophy & Ethics

This module is devoted to yogic philosophy which gives in-depth knowledge of types of yoga schools. An introduction to Patanjali Sutras is foundational towards becoming a yoga teacher. The study includes Tridosha and Pancha Prana, Yogic Ethics and Guidelines, Koshas and Chakras, and the concept of Kundalini with associated aspects.

6

YOGA MODULE 6: MEDITATION

Mind Training

Covers scientific aspects of meditation as well as practical classroom practice of a variety of meditation techniques. You will experience and learn to teach Yoga Nidra, Chakra Meditation, Soham Meditation and silent meditation practices within a structured, progressive framework.

7

YOGA MODULE 7: ANATOMY & PHYSIOLOGY

Body Science

Anatomy: The study of structure & framework of the human body. Physiology: The study of organ systems & how they function in coordination.

This module is theoretical and enables the yoga teacher to connect the knowledge of yoga to the body. The syllabus covers the musculoskeletal system, the respiratory and cardiovascular systems, and the nervous system in relation to yoga.

8

YOGA MODULE 8: PROFESSIONAL SKILLS

Teaching Excellence

Comprehensive training to develop professional teaching skills and business acumen for yoga instructors. You’ll refine employability skills, the student–teacher relationship, lesson planning and sequencing, the business aspects of yoga, and all areas of class and student management.

9

YOGA MODULE 9: PRACTICUM

Hands-on Application

This module is devoted to the practical display of knowledge, understanding, application, skills and experiences across the whole syllabus. You will conduct teaching practice sessions, receive peer feedback and assessment, and demonstrate real-world application as part of your final certification assessment and portfolio.

"Teaching Yoga, Touching Lives"

As part of our 500-Hour Professional Yoga Teacher Training, students engage in advanced community service through specialized yoga sessions, therapeutic outreach, and mentorship activities. This professional-level experience allows you to practice advanced teaching skills while making a meaningful impact in your community.

On completion, you will be awarded an Advanced Community Service Certificate recognizing your professional contribution to spreading wellness and advancing the yogic lifestyle in your community.
