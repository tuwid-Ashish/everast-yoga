Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

Deliver rooted, evidence-aware yoga education that transforms students into compassionate practitioners and effective teachers.

Vision

Make authentic yoga accessible globally—integrating tradition, modern pedagogy and Indian wellness ethos.

Values
  • Parampara (lineage) with scientific rigor
  • Safety-first sequencing & therapy orientation
  • Inclusivity, professionalism, and seva (service)
