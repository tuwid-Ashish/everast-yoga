12-Week Journey

Your Training Timeline

A structured 12-week journey that transforms you from student to certified instructor.

Weeks 1-3: Foundation Building

Master the fundamentals, anatomy basics, and core principles of Pilates.

Weeks 4-6: Mat Work Mastery

Complete classical mat repertoire with modifications and progressions.

Weeks 7-9: Equipment Training

Hands-on training with Reformer, Cadillac, Chair, and accessories.

Weeks 10-11: Teaching Practice

Develop teaching skills, practice cueing, and work with clients.

Week 12: Certification

Final assessments, practical exams, and certificate presentation.
